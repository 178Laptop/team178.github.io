Meet our mentors and coaches!

We are very grateful to have many mentors be a part of our team. We love to say that we are mentor nurtured and student driven. Our 12 mentors, 50% who are Enforcer alumni, may not directly be leading projects. However, they all are essential in giving their insight and expertise to the team. They have also been able to provide us with many resources that have been essential in the functioning of our team. Without our mentors, our team would not be able to run! Thank you to all our mentors!
