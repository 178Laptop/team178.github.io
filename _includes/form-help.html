Help with Forms

You must print the forms out, fill them out legibly, scan them into your computer, turn them into a single .pdf file, upload that file to Google Drive, and share it with Trips@FarmingtonRobotics.org.

How to scan papers and turn them into a .pdf

You can either use your home printer/scanner to turn your forms into .pdfs or you can use a mobile app. There is one for Android and one for iOS (iPhones & iPods).
